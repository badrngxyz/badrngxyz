The blog's name is no accident. 😅 I consider myself an unlucky person who tries to explore diverse activities, from whittling to skydiving. However, I struggle with a near-crippling perfectionism that prevents me from sharing anything that isn't logically flawless and airtight.

Here, I'm challenging myself to share more, recognizing that logical perfection is unattainable and that few absolute rights and wrongs exist in the human experience. Life is an incredible journey, and this blog will document my personal wanderings.

I'm also a software developer driven by curiosity and a desire to experiment. While this makes it difficult to settle into one area, it allows me to borrow concepts from different languages to create elegant software. The craft itself keeps me engaged: tweaking flows, applying idioms, streamlining architecture, striving for simplicity, or pursuing anything that delivers that satisfying sense of accomplishment.

Although I'm Brazilian, I write in English to practice and connect with a broader audience. As a result, errors are inevitable, and not every post will resonate with everyone. However, I welcome constructive feedback and encourage you to reach out on GitHub by opening an issue.
